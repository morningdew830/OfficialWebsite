We are looking for you!

Working at Brightergy means you’ll be working with colleagues who are bright, ambitious, and passionate about reimagining the world of energy. We look to hire people who are motivated self-starters with positive “can-do” attitudes, and who are looking for an opportunity to improve their professional career through hard work, creativity, and dedication.

If that’s you, browse our open positions to see what might be a good fit for your skill set and interests. We look forward to hearing from you.

Photography Interen - St Louis

Location: Kansas City

Department Name: Field Operations

Position: Lighting Project Auditor

Department: Operations

Reports To: Estimator/CSS

As a member of the Operations Team, the Lighting Project Auditor shares in the overall team responsibility to provide quality customer service to our clients, vendors and anyone else involved in the life of a project.

The role of the Lighting Project Auditor is to accurately collect specific lighting information from prospective client site locations.

Often, the Lighting Project Auditor is the face of Brightergy during the initial contact with the client. As such, this position is expected to be professional over the phone and in person with all Clients and Brightergy team members. The role must have a collaborative approach to working with Clients and other team members. The goal is to understand, organize, and execute data collection for lighting retrofit projects.

Specific Responsibilities:
  • * Learn Audit Process and work with Sales to outline and define audit scope requirements
  • * Assist in quotation, proposal and bid presentation
  • * Scheduling and coordination of audit trips
  • * Preparing detailed audit reports summarizing audit results "fixture counts/types, existing conditions and surroundings"
  • * Maintain accurate account of audit schedules/status in current Company CRM (Salesforce)
  • * Proactively identify and communicate issues to appropriate operations staff as they arise
  • * Travel to project sites
  • * Attend training programs as required
  • * Additional duties as assigned
