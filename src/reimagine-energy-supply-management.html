REGULATED RATE ANALYSIS

Brightergy Regulated Rate Analysis evaluates and recommends the optimum tariff structure in regulated service territories. In addition to assessing the various utility rates and tariff options, we evaluate demand response and other demand mitigation opportunities to drive energy savings.

Just because you’re in a regulated utility market with a monopoly energy provider doesn’t mean there aren’t opportunities to realize energy savings by restructuring and consolidating meters, accounts, and/or tariffs.
