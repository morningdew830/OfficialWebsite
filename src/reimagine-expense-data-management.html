EXPENSE AND DATA MANAGEMENT

Brightergy expense and data management means you can think of us as your energy department, as we work with you to develop a comprehensive energy strategy tailored to your specific circumstances.

UTILITY MANAGEMENT & REPORTING

This monthly service allows you to focus on what you do best – run your organization – without having to worry about the accuracy of your energy, water, and wastewater bills. Brightergy energy experts transform your energy data into insights that can help your decision-making.

HOW IT WORKS

We receive your bills directly from your supplier and audit them to

  • Ensure accuracy of the utility price
  • Review them for taxes
  • Review for late payment fees
  • Review for direct and indirect emissions
  • Identify and correct any billing errors
  • Identify and isolate any issues or opportunities – our team then finds and implements a solution

Your audited bills are forwarded to you for both payment and peace of mind. In addition, we generate a detailed, customized report each month tracking trends and providing a comparative analysis relating to:

  • Facility consumption
  • Peak usage
  • Cost-per-unit of production
  • Contract status and alternatives
  • Resolution tracking
  • Any other data points you select
