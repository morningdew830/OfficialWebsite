This is Brightergy

why we do what we do

We believe in a world where we can all thrive through energy independence. This means reimagining the way we generate, consume, and manage energy today.

Technology changes all the time. What made sense a 100 years ago isn’t necessarily the best strategy for us today. And we all need energy - to teach, to create solutions, to lead, and to live. Not only will this never change, but it will only continue to grow.

Technologies like solar energy and combined heat and power now make it possible for us to generate energy where we need it. This is distributed energy.

Equipment such as light bulbs and heating and cooling systems are increasingly made to use less energy. This is efficiency.

The Internet of Things makes it possible to connect a vast network of devices, unlocking vast energy data that you can use to benefit both your bottom line and your community. This is intelligence.

In short, we can own our energy in a way we’ve never been able to before.

This is powerful. It’s the makings of a new world, where energy is no longer a liability or an expense, but an asset - a world where people aren’t limited by their access - or ties - to the grid.

Join us in the movement to Reimagine Energy.

WHAT WE DO

our story

2003

Lots of great companies started in a garage. Ours was in St. Louis.

In 2003, the Energy Savings Store opened as a two-person operation in a St. Louis garage to bring solar power to area homes and business

2010

Rebirth as Brightergy

Entrepreneur and KC native Adam Blake met TESS’s Ryan Gardner for lunch in July 2010. Their shared ideas led to the deal that formed Brightergy.

2011

Beyond solar

In 2011, the price of solar panels began a dramatic decline, as global adoption grew, and a vision began to take shape for a Brightergy that offered more than just solar power, and more than just solutions.

Reimagine energy

Technology changes all the time, but our need for energy never will. We see a world in which we all thrive through energy independence. Through utility tax management, energy efficiency, our intelligence platform, solar power, and more, we empower organizations to take control of their energy, to turn it from a liability to an asset, to save both dollars and emissions.
